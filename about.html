About Us

Finding Our Voice is a program dedicated to supporting newcomer students in their journey to learn English and succeed academically. We work closely with multilingual teachers, parents, and communities to foster inclusivity and empowerment through education.

Our Mission

We believe that language should never be a barrier to success. Our mission is to provide resources, mentorship, and innovative teaching methods to help students achieve their full potential.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: We celebrate diversity and ensure every student feels valued.
  • Empowerment: Providing tools and resources for students to succeed.
  • Collaboration: Building strong partnerships with teachers, families, and communities.

Our Impact

Since our inception, we have empowered over 500 students to achieve their academic and language goals. Through workshops, mentorship programs, and innovative teaching methods, we strive to make a lasting impact in the lives of our students and their communities.
